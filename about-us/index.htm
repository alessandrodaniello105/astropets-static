Behind the project

We are Vhor

At Vhor Technologies, we believe that empowering gamers through incorporating WEB-3 technologies into the present video game industry is not a proposal, but a duty. We strive to develop new technologies in tandem with exceptional innovation in order to deliver one-of-a-kind projects, enhanced gaming experiences, and gameplay exclusivity to our customers.

Mission
By innovating the current video game industry, we aspire to provide unique experiences for players from all over the world.
Vision
Through our gaming software, we strive to empower users from all walks of life by developing and enabling new revenue streams.
